DELHI TAEKWONDO SPORTS ACADEMY (D.T.S.A.) - Building Champions Since 2010

THE D.T.S.A. DIFFERENCE

Founded in 2012, DELHI TAEKWONDO SPORTS ACADEMY (D.T.S.A.) has established itself as the premier martial arts training center in Delhi. Our academy combines authentic Korean techniques with modern training methodologies to create champions both in competition and in life.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We live by the five sacred tenets of Taekwondo: Courtesy, Integrity, Perseverance, Self-control, and Indomitable Spirit. At D.T.S.A., we don't just teach fighting techniques – we forge unbreakable character and warrior spirit.

WORLD-CLASS FACILITIES

Train in our state-of-the-art dojang featuring Olympic-standard mats, advanced impact training equipment, and specialized conditioning zones designed for optimal martial arts development and safety.
